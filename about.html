About GreenPepper

GreenPepper is a collaboration platform to help business experts and developers create executable specificatons in order to build the right software.
It was brought to Life by Pyxis , supported for some years, and opensourced by them. Currently no one is providing anymore support to it while some projects have invested so much in it.
We decided to revive GreenPepper and we need YOUR help for doing so.
